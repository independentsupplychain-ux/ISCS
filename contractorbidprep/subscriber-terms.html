1. Scope of Service

Contractors Bid Prep ("CBP") provides government bid monitoring and administrative preparation services to subscribers under one of the following tiers:

  • Bronze: Weekly bid opportunity scanning and SMS alerts for one trade category, with up to 5 active opportunities tracked.
  • Silver: Everything in Bronze, plus automated bid document generation, e-signature workflow, up to 3 trade categories, and client portal access.
  • Gold: Everything in Silver, plus unlimited trade categories and active opportunities, custom bid document templates, priority support, and a monthly strategy call.

The specific features included in your plan are as described on the CBP pricing page at the time of your enrollment.

2. No Guarantee / Disclaimer of Results

CBP provides monitoring, notification, and administrative preparation services only. We do not guarantee that you will receive any particular number of bid alerts, that any bid you submit will be awarded, or that any specific financial outcome will result from your subscription. Past performance of CBP or other subscribers is not indicative of future results.

3. Contractor Responsibilities

  • You are solely responsible for confirming your eligibility, licensure, bonding, and insurance for any opportunity you choose to bid on.
  • You are responsible for the accuracy and completeness of any bid amount, scope, or supporting information you provide to CBP.
  • You agree to maintain an active, valid contractor license for your trade for the duration of your subscription.
  • You agree to review all bid documents prepared on your behalf before they are submitted or signed.

4. Payment Terms

  • Subscription fees are billed monthly or annually, as selected at enrollment, via Stripe.
  • All subscription fees are non-refundable once a billing period has begun.
  • A setup fee may apply to certain plans; CBP may waive the setup fee at its sole discretion.
  • CBP will provide at least 30 days' written notice of any change to subscription pricing. Continued use of the service after a price change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the new pricing.

5. SMS Consent & TCPA

SMS notifications are a core feature of certain CBP plans. Consent to receive SMS messages is collected separately at enrollment and is not a condition of purchasing any CBP service, in compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

  • Reply STOP to any message at any time to opt out of SMS notifications.
  • Opt-out requests will be honored within 10 business days.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

6. Data Handling

Subscriber data, including bid records and enrollment information, is stored using Notion and DocuSeal. CBP does not sell subscriber data to third parties. Please note that bid submissions made through public government procurement processes may become public records under applicable Florida and Alabama public records laws, regardless of how CBP stores your data.

CBP retains exclusive ownership of all bid document templates, automation workflows, and proprietary processes used to deliver the service. Subscribers are granted a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use generated bid documents solely for the purpose of submitting bids during their active subscription.
