SMS Communications Consent

By enrolling in a Contractor Bid Prep (CBP) service plan, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Contractor Bid Prep at the phone number provided during enrollment. Messages will include alerts about government bid opportunities matching your trade and, for full-service clients, requests to provide your bid amount for a specific opportunity.

You will not receive messages unless you are an active subscriber to a CBP service plan. SMS notifications are a core feature of your subscription, not a separate marketing opt-in.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the volume of bid opportunities identified for your trade and service area in a given week. Most subscribers can expect between 1 and 10 messages per week.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opting Out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from SMS notifications. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out of SMS may limit certain features of your service plan (such as two-way bid response). For questions, contact hello@contractorbidprep.com.

Terms of Service

Our Service

Contractor Bid Prep monitors public government procurement portals across Florida and Alabama for bid opportunities relevant to your trade and service area. Depending on your plan, CBP may notify you of relevant opportunities, prepare bid documentation, and route documents for your signature.

No Guarantee of Award

CBP provides monitoring, notification, and administrative preparation services. We do not guarantee that any bid opportunity will result in a contract award. You are solely responsible for the accuracy, completeness, and competitiveness of any bid amount or document you submit.

Subscription & Billing

  • CBP plans (Bronze, Silver, and Gold) are billed monthly unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • Setup fees, where applicable, will be disclosed prior to enrollment.

Your Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate contact information, including a valid mobile number for SMS notifications.
  • Review all bid documents prepared on your behalf before signing.
  • Maintain any licenses, bonding, or insurance required to bid on a given opportunity.

Changes to These Terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Refund Policy

All payments to Contractor Bid Prep are non-refundable. When you purchase a subscription — monthly or annual — you are paying for immediate access to the CBP platform and bid monitoring service. That access begins at the moment of purchase.

We do not issue refunds for unused portions of a billing period, partial months, or annual plans. If you cancel your subscription, your access continues through the end of the current paid period and does not renew.

If you believe you were charged in error, contact us at davidduncan@contractorbidprep.com and we will review your case.

Privacy

Contractor Bid Prep collects the contact and business information necessary to operate your subscription, including your name, business name, phone number, email address, and trade/license information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send bid opportunity alerts and process your bid responses.
  • To prepare and route bid documents for signature.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell your phone number or personal information to third parties. Mobile opt-in information and consent are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I stop receiving text messages?

Reply STOP to any CBP message, or email hello@contractorbidprep.com to update your notification preferences.

What happens if I don't reply to a bid alert?

No further action is taken on that opportunity. You'll continue to receive alerts for future opportunities matching your trade.

Can I change my service plan?

Yes. Contact us at hello@contractorbidprep.com to upgrade, downgrade, or adjust your plan.
