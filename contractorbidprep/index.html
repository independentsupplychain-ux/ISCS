How Contractor Bid Prep Works

From bid alert to signed contract — we automate every step.

1

We Scan the Portals

Every week our system scans state and local procurement channels for new solicitations matching your trade and geography.

2

You Get a Text

When a matching opportunity drops, you get an SMS alert and email with the project name, agency, and any other relevant information — right to your phone and mailbox.

3

Reply with Your Number

Reply with your bid amount and any other required information. Our system automatically generates the bid documents from your response.

4

Sign & Submit

A document link hits your inbox. Sign digitally, and your completed bid package is ready to submit — often same day.
