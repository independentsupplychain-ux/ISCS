How Contractor Bid Prep Works

From bid alert to signed contract — we automate every step.

1

We Scan the Portals

Every week our system scrapes DemandStar, MFMP, and local government portals for new solicitations matching your trade and geography.

2

You Get a Text

When a matching opportunity drops, you get an SMS alert with the project name, agency, and estimated value — right to your phone.

3

Reply with Your Number

Text back your bid amount. That's it. Our system automatically generates the bid documents from your response.

4

Sign & Submit

A PandaDoc link hits your inbox. Sign digitally, and your completed bid package is ready to submit — often within the hour.
